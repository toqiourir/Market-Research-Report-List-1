<p><p>Refrigerated warehouses can be categorized into two main types: public and private. Public refrigerated warehouses are third-party facilities that offer storage services to multiple clients, enabling companies to access cold storage without significant capital investment. These warehouses typically operate on a pay-per-use basis. In contrast, private refrigerated warehouses are owned and operated by a single company, tailored to specific needs, providing greater control over storage conditions and processes, but requiring higher initial costs and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
