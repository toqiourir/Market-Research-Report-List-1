<p><p>The electric control valve market consists of several types based on design functionality. Single-way valves allow fluid flow in one direction, mainly used for on/off control. Two-way valves have two ports, enabling direct flow control and isolation between two systems. Three-way valves feature three ports, facilitating mixing or diverting flows, suitable for complex applications. Other valve types include multi-port and pressure-regulating valves, catering to specialized needs in various industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
