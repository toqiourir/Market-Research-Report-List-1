<p><p>The fire extinguisher cylinders market includes various types such as dry chemical, foam, carbon dioxide, water, and others. Dry chemical extinguishers effectively combat flammable liquid and gas fires using a powder agent. Foam extinguishers are designed for liquid fires, forming a blanket to suffocate flames. Carbon dioxide extinguishers displace oxygen to extinguish fires, primarily in electronic equipment. Water extinguishers cool and extinguish fires involving solid combustibles. Other types may include specialized extinguishers like wet chemical for deep fat fires or clean agent extinguishers.</p></p>
