<p><strong>The Laser Interferometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homodyne Laser Interferometer</li><li>Heterodyne Laser Interferometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser interferometer market comprises mainly two types: homodyne and heterodyne laser interferometers. Homodyne laser interferometers utilize a single frequency light source, providing high precision and stability for measuring small displacements. They are ideal for applications requiring extreme accuracy, such as optics and manufacturing. In contrast, heterodyne laser interferometers use two different frequency sources, allowing them to measure larger distances with improved measurement speed. This versatility makes them suitable for applications in industrial automation and aerospace testing.</p></p>
