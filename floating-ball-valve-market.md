<p><p>A floating ball valve is a type of quarter-turn valve that features a spherical disc (the ball) which floats within the valve body. This design allows the ball to seat against the valve's downstream seat when pressure is applied, providing a tight seal and preventing leakage. These valves are widely used in industrial applications for their reliability and ease of operation.</p><p>The Floating Ball Valve Market is projected to grow significantly, expected to expand at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period.
