<p><ul><li>Transmission substations</li><li>Distribution substations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The substation automation market is primarily divided into two types: transmission substations and distribution substations. Transmission substations facilitate the bulk transfer of high-voltage electricity over long distances, requiring advanced automation for monitoring and control to ensure reliability and efficiency. Conversely, distribution substations focus on distributing lower voltage electricity to end consumers, emphasizing automation for enhanced operational efficiency and grid management. Both market segments are driven by the need for improved safety, reliability, and integration of renewable energy sources.</p></p>
