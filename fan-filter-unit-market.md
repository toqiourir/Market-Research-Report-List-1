<p><p>The Fan Filter Unit (FFU) market is primarily categorized into two types based on the filtration medium: FFUs with HEPA filters and those with ULPA filters. FFUs with HEPA filters efficiently capture particles down to 0.3 microns, making them suitable for environments requiring high cleanliness levels, such as pharmaceuticals and data centers. In contrast, FFUs with ULPA filters provide even higher filtration efficiency, targeting particles as small as 0.1 microns, ideal for more stringent applications in aerospace and biotechnology sectors.</p></p>
