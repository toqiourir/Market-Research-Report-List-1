<p><ul><li>Steam and Water Spray Retort</li><li>Water Immersion Retort</li><li>Air Steam Retort</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static retorts are classified into three main types based on their heating methods: Steam and Water Spray Retorts, Water Immersion Retorts, and Air Steam Retorts. </p><p>Steam and Water Spray Retorts use a combination of steam and pressurized water to effectively sterilize food. Water Immersion Retorts fully submerge containers in heated water for uniform temperature distribution. Air Steam Retorts utilize a mixture of air and steam to achieve higher temperatures, enhancing sterilization. Each type serves specific food processing requirements, ensuring safety and extending shelf life.</p></p>
