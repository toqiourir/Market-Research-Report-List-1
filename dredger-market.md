<p><strong>The Dredger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cutter-Suction Dredger</li><li>Trailing Suction Hopper Dredger</li><li>Chain Bucket Dredge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dredger market encompasses various specialized equipment types for marine excavation. The Cutter-Suction Dredger (CSD) uses a rotating cutter head to detach sediment, then pumps it through a pipeline. The Trailing Suction Hopper Dredger (TSHD) features suction pipes that draw material into onboard hoppers for transport. The Chain Bucket Dredge employs a series of buckets on a continuous chain to scoop up material. Each type serves distinct purposes in dredging operations, influencing market dynamics based on project requirements and efficiency.</p></p>
