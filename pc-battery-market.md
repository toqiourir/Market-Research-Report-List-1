<p><p>The PC battery market consists of three main types: Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries, known for their durability but suffering from memory effect; Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries, which offer improved capacity and are more environmentally friendly than NiCd; and Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries, favored for their high energy density, lighter weight, and longer lifespan. Each type serves different needs, with Li-ion dominating due to its efficiency and performance in modern computing devices, while NiCd and NiMH are gradually being phased out.</p></p>
