<p><ul><li>Frame Mounted End Suction Pump</li><li>Close Coupled End Suction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal end suction pump market is segmented into two main types: frame mounted end suction pumps and close coupled end suction pumps. Frame mounted end suction pumps are designed as separate assemblies, providing flexibility for installation and maintenance in various industrial applications. In contrast, close coupled end suction pumps feature a compact design, directly connecting the motor to the pump, which reduces overall space requirements and simplifies installation. Both types cater to diverse sectors, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
