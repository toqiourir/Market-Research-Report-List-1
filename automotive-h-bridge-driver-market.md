<p><strong>The Automotive H Bridge Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single H-Bridge</li><li>Dual H-Bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive H-Bridge driver market consists of two primary types: Single H-Bridge and Dual H-Bridge configurations. A Single H-Bridge controls one motor or actuator, making it suitable for simpler applications like electric windows or mirrors. In contrast, a Dual H-Bridge can control two motors or allow for bidirectional movement, making it ideal for more complex systems such as electric steering or traction control. The choice between these types depends on the application requirements and desired functionality in vehicles.</p></p>
