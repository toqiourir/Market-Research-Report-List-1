<p><p>The hydraulic impact wrench market primarily consists of two types: traditional impact wrenches and pulse pneumatic wrenches. Traditional impact wrenches use a rotating hammer mechanism to deliver high torque for heavy-duty applications, making them ideal for tasks requiring significant force. Pulse pneumatic wrenches, on the other hand, utilize a pulse mechanism that provides controlled torque with less vibration and noise, suitable for precision work. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and construction, enhancing efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
