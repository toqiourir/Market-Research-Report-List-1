<p><strong>The Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multimode Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber coupler market is primarily divided into two types: single mode couplers and multimode couplers. Single mode couplers are designed for use with single mode fibers, allowing the propagation of light through a single path, which enhances performance over long distances. Multimode couplers, on the other hand, support multiple light paths through multimode fibers, making them suitable for shorter distances and applications where high bandwidth is needed. Each type serves distinct applications in telecommunications and data transmission.</p></p>
