<p><strong>The Durometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brinell Durometer</li><li>Vickers Durometer</li><li>Rockwell Durometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The durometer market is segmented into various types based on hardness measurement methods. Brinell Durometers assess materials by measuring the indentation produced by a hard ball, ideal for larger samples. Vickers Durometers utilize a diamond pyramid indenter, suitable for all hardness levels, providing versatility. Rockwell Durometers measure the depth of indentation under a specific load, often employed for quick assessments. Other durometer types include Shore durometers and Leeb hardness testers, catering to diverse industrial applications across material testing and quality control.</p></p>
