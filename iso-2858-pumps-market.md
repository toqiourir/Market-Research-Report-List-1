<p><p>The ISO 2858 pumps market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on magdrive and sealed pumps. Magdrive pumps utilize magnetic coupling to eliminate the need for shaft seals, reducing leakage and maintenance issues, making them suitable for hazardous liquids. Sealed pumps, on the other hand, employ traditional mechanical seals to prevent fluid leakage, providing a cost-effective solution for standard applications. Both types address diverse industrial needs, offering reliability and efficiency in fluid handling across various sectors.</p></p>
